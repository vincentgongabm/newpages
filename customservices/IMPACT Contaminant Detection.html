Contamination of biological samples such as tumor cells, hybridomas, and viruses used on research animals can cause disease outbreaks or affect the integrity and outcome of results. abm uses the advanced Infectious Microbe PCR AmplifiCation Test (IMPACT) testing method to detect a wide-range of known contaminants. This method is superior to the traditional Mouse Antibody Production (MAP) test in the following ways:

  1. Turnaround time in as little as 1-2 weeks
  2. PCR test result does not rely on mouse immune response
  3. PCR testing does not require the use of live animals
  4. PCR testing is more sensitive and can detect smaller amounts of infectants

Our preselected contaminant detection service options include the most common viruses and Mycoplasma species that might afflict mice, giving reliable and quick results.

Sample Submission Requirements:
Biological Samples / Cell Culture:
Please send in 1 cryovial per sample containing at least 2x106 cells/vial. We accept samples in cell pellet form, in growth medium, freeze medium, or phosphate-buffered saline.

Liquid Samples:
Please send in 1 cryovial per sample with a minimum volume of 0.5ml for best results; however, if sample is limited, smaller amounts can also be accommodated. Please contact us for details.

*All samples should be collected under sterile conditions. Please ship samples with a sufficient amount of dry ice in order to keep them frozen.
