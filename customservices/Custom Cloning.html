A one-stop source for research solutions, abm offers gene synthesis, cloning, and virus packaging services aimed to reduce the time, effort and money spent by researchers.

abm's top-notch custom services include, but are not limited to:

  • subcloning
  • gene synthesis
  • codon optimization (upon request)
  • mutagenesis
  • expression vector customization: tissue-specific promoters; fluorescent reporters; tags and lox sites; drug resistance markers
  • sequence verification of inserts in final vector; vector verification by restriction digest

Our experienced scientists will assemble your clone accurately, rapidly, economically, and ready to use for your experiment.
