abm’s custom retroviruses are a highly-efficient method to achieve stable gene expression in a broad range of dividing mammalian cells. Simply send us your gene, or purchase an ORF vector from our extensive library of human, mouse, or rat genes, and we will do the rest! Our packaged retroviruses are available with your choice of no tag, HA tag, or GFP reporter.

KEY FEATURES OF ABM'S CUSTOM RETROVIRUS SERVICE
Promoter CMV promoter for strong gene expression in most cellular systems
Tags Available No tag
HA tag for detection or protein purification
GFP reporter
Retovirus TIters Up to 109 IU/ml
Safety Replication-incompetent retrovirus with minimal relation to the wild-type Moloney Murine Leukemia Virus (MoMuLV)

FAQs

How long does it take to produce virus?
This will be dependent on a number of factors, including the plasmid you would like to package. Please send all inquiries to technical@abmgood.com for a prompt response on the estimated lead time.
What the concentration of transfer vector should I submit?
The volume of DNA needed to produce recombinant virus has been listed alongside each titre available. (see above)
How do you determine titer?
Via RT-PCR
What is the packaging capacity for Retrovirus?
The maximum insert size is <8 kb between the 5'LTR to 3'LTR.
What is the minimum amount of DNA needed for subcloning into a retroviral vector (C057)?
The minimum amount of DNA we require is 10μg for subcloning into a retroviral vector.
