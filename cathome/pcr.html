As an industry-leading and global biotech company, abm offers a wide range of ISO13485 certified PCR, RT-PCR and qPCR enzyme products suitable for diagnostic use.

  • PCR: Over 10 strategically engineered DNA polymerases are available, offered in enzyme-only and 2X PCR MasterMix formats.
  • RT: Two supreme OneScript® Reverse Transcriptases offered as All-In-One RT MasterMix, RT SuperMix, One-Step cDNA Synthesis Kits, and One-Step qRT-PCR Kits.
  • qPCR: Dye-based 2X and 5X qPCR MasterMixes suitable for standard and fast cycling conditions, as well as specially formulated MasterMixes suitable for miRNA and long amplicons. TaqProbe-based 2X and 5X qPCR MasterMixes are also available.
  • Nucleic Acid Staining: Developed to replace toxic Ethidium Bromide, SafeView™ products are a new class of nucleic acid stains for the visualization of double-stranded DNA, single-stranded DNA, and RNA in both agarose and polyacrylamide gels.
  • Specialized Kits: Real-time qPCR detection kits of viral particles for recombinant lentivirus, retrovirus and AAV, as well as PCR-based mycoplasma detection Kit for cell culture applications. Crude sample preparation kits for tissues, plants, blood or cell samples are also available.
