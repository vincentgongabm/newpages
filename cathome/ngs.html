Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) is the answer to decipher the potential locked in our genetic material and has come to represent a monumental technological advancement that is redefining many aspects of scientific research, with direct applications to biology, personalized medicine, agriculture, and environmental research. ABM, an Illumina Certified Service Provider delivers high throughput, high scalability, and high reliability NGS services. With superior technical support and expertise, abm can customize any workflow to fit your NGS project.
