For gene regulation and functional studies, abm offers a variety of expression systems for:

  1. siRNA: efficiently express any target siRNA to knockdown any gene without the need to design hair-pin loop shRNA structures, available in lentivirus, AAV and Adenovirus.
  2. miRNA: inhibit or over-express any miRNA for studies of post-translational gene regulation in mammalian systems using our ready-to-use viral vectors and packaged viruses, as well as our detection and quantification tools.
  3. UTR Reporters: quantitatively study a specific miRNA's regulation of its target gene using our 3’UTR or 5’UTR platform, all available as a library of premade lentiviral vectors and lentiviruses for any human, mouse or rat gene.
